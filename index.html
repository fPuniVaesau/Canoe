Who am I, really?

Im Filo Puni Vaesau and I am the creator of this project. Welcome To Canoe. This is a project I created to help document my personal journey as a self taught software developer.

In addition, this project is for others willing to share thier journey as well. We all strive to find our own path in life and in doing so we craft our own story to tell.

Hopefully from this project, you can leverage not only my personal story, but the stories of all our incredible guests to tip the scale in your favor as you embark on a journey that is your own.

Be encouraged, Be inspired, Be yourself. Goodluck!
